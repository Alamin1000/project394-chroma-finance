Secure & Diversified Asset Management.

Chroma Finance is a diversified investment fund providing clients with access to talented and experienced asset management across multiple financial markets and asset classes without the usual hinderence of exorbitant fees and/or high barriers of entry.

Innovative Asset management

By diversifying across multiple financial markets and asset classes we provide highly innovative and broad investment management which provably reduces risk and increases profit potential compared to other more linear investments.

All in one

Chroma has only one main investment pool. All investment strategies used by Chroma can be used with up to over $100m AUM with negligable impact to profit potential.

Fair management fees

We incorporate a 10% deposit fee and a 30% performance fee on all profits generated by our investments. All withdrawals are fee-free.

2-factor authentication

Chroma provides a typical 2-factor authentication system to enhance the security of client accounts on our platform. 2-factor authentication is required to invest with Chroma.

Accessability

We accept over 14 cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum,
BNB, USDT, and more.

65% Cryptocurrency derivatives trading

25% Forex leverage trading

These trading strategies include both manual and automatic strategies, on a large range of pairs and leverage levels. We primarily use the FBS broker for our forex trading activities. Note: some automated forex strategies are not built by the Chroma team. These are run by developers using the MT4 platform through the Chroma team's brokerage account.

10% Long term investments

We hold a range of long term cryptocurrency investments. All of these investments are held in cold storage and secured by Ledger. Long term investments function as on-hand liquidity for extra margin.
